About Kumasi Hospital

Kumasi Hospital, Located in the heart of Sokoban-Ampayoo,Kumasi is a leading healthcare institution in Ghana, providing comprehensive medical services with state-of-the-art technology and a team of dedicated healthcare professionals.

Founded in 2020, we have been serving the community with excellence in healthcare. we have been at the forefront of medical excellence, serving not just the Ashanti Region but patients from across Ghana and neighboring countries.

Our hospital boasts a comprehensive range of medical services delivered through cutting-edge technology and a team of highly specialized healthcare professionals. We have invested heavily in modern medical equipment, including advanced diagnostic imaging systems, fully automated laboratory equipment, and digital patient management systems that ensure seamless healthcare delivery.

OUR MISSION is to bring standard care within the reach of every individual, building a healthier future together.

OUR VISION is to provide an excellent patient care experience everytime at an affordable cost
